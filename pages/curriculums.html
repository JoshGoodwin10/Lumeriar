CAPS-Aligned Curriculums

  • Structured learning pathways from Grade R to Grade 12

Comprehensive Robotics Education

Our curriculums are meticulously designed to align with the South African CAPS framework, ensuring that robotics and coding education integrates seamlessly with existing educational standards. From foundational concepts in early grades to advanced programming and AI applications in high school, we provide a complete learning journey.

Each curriculum module includes detailed lesson plans, student worksheets, assessment rubrics, and teacher guides to ensure successful implementation in any classroom setting.

Curriculum by Grade Level

Tailored learning experiences for every stage of development
Grade R-3

Foundation Phase

Introduction to basic robotics concepts through play-based learning. Students explore simple machines, sequences, and problem-solving using age-appropriate kits and activities.

  • Visual block-based programming
  • Hands-on building activities
  • STEAM integration
Grade 4-6

Intermediate Phase

Development of computational thinking and basic coding skills. Students design and program robots to solve real-world challenges while learning about sensors, motors, and logic.

  • Introduction to sensors & actuators
  • Block-based to text-based coding
  • Project-based learning
Grade 7-9

Senior Phase

Advanced programming concepts, robotics design principles, and introduction to AI fundamentals. Students work on complex projects involving multiple systems and autonomous behaviors.

  • Python & text-based programming
  • Advanced robotics concepts
  • Team-based challenges
Grade 10-12

FET Phase

Industry-aligned vocational training in robotics, AI, and automation. Students gain practical skills in machine learning, computer vision, and advanced system integration preparing them for tertiary education or employment.

  • Machine learning basics
  • Industry partnerships
  • Portfolio development
